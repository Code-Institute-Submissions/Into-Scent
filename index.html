Our Workshops

Our workshops are designed to give you an insight into the use and get a glimpse of the world of Aromatherapy. Each workshop covers "introduction to essential oils, carrier oils and safe guidance, basic aromatherapy techniques, different application methods and massage.

We are also happy to arrange a workshop for groups or private events. Please contact us for more details.

Terms & Conditions

Welcome to Into Scent. These terms and conditions apply to services supplied to you by us.

Payment

All workshops must be paid in one payment to secure the booking.

Late arrivals

If you might arrive late for your workshop, please call us as soon as possible. However, you will not be able to attend the workshop if you are more than 10 minutes late.
The Tutors will be expected to start the workshop on time and will not be able to repeat any parts of the workshop for late arrivals. No refunds will be available to late arrivals as per our bookings and cancellation policy below.

Bookings and cancellations for individuals

If you need to cancel or reschedule a booking, please notify us by emailing us at least 24 hours prior to the date of your workshop booked. If you cancel or reschedule a booking within 24 hours of your workshop booked, we will not be able to refund of the fee or reschedule.

Bookings and cancellations for group bookings

If you need to cancel or reschedule a booking, please notify us by phoning us at least 7 days prior to the date of your workshop booked. We will refund you the fee in full. If you cancel or reschedule less than 7 days of your workshop booked, we are unfortunately unable to offer the refund of the fee or rescheduling.

Eligibility

Please inform us in advance if you have any illnesses, medical conditions, medications, injuries or allergies or if you are pregnant. This may affect your suitability for attending certain workshops.
